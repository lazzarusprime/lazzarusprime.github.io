⌨️ Keyboard & Help shortcuts · remapping · recording
Keyboard: defaults to QWERTY — Q Closed Hat · W Open Hat · E Tom High · R Tom Low · T Floor Tom · Y Crash · U Ride · I Splash · O Cowbell · P Clap · A Snare · Space Kick. Your numpad works too, all the time, laid out to match (Numpad5 Kick, Numpad2 Snare, etc.) — it's a bonus and isn't affected by remapping. Pads show both by default; change that under Customize Kit → Key Labels. Custom layout: pick "Custom (Drag to Arrange)" under Customize Kit and drag any pad wherever you want it; positions save automatically. Record captures whatever you play (up to 30s) — hit Play to hear it back once, or turn on Loop to keep it cycling. Clear resets the take. Save keeps your last recording in this browser so it survives a refresh; Load brings it back. Export downloads the take as a .json file to back up or share; Import loads one back in. Kit size, layout, shell finish and key bindings are all remembered next time you visit.
